League Rules

  • Each owner has a roster of players.
  • Weekly Scoring: Only the best 3 adjusted scores count toward the team total.
  • Underdogs: Designated players receive a 0.5x multiplier on their placement (lower is better).
  • Event Multipliers: Majors (2x), Elite Series/Silver (1.5x), others (1x).
  • Lower total cumulative score wins the season.
